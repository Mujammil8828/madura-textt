Corporate Social Responsibility

Apart from pioneers in multiple sectors, Kalra Group is also committed towards its social responsibilities. The Group has shown significiant concern towards environment and community.

Towards Environment

As an initiative towards a greener tomorrow, Kalra Group has started using modern and technologically advanced production equipments to minimize its ecological footprints. Along with that the Group has also shifted to Natural gas instead of oil for its energy requirements reducing the damage caused to the environment.

For a sustainable tomorrow MITL manufacturing has 30% of green areas making it a better place to work for the workers and for the society as a whole.

Towards Community

The Kalra Group is focused on supporting elementary education and skills development for the children of workers at MITL.

The Kalra Group is committed and focused in imparting education and contributes in the development of the nation.
