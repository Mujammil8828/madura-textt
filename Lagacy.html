Legacy

In business over 100 years, MITL is proud of its legacy. We played a key role in developing not just the Industrial Textiles sector, but the Indian economy at large.

2012 - 13

Commissioned a Green Feild Project at Sarigram with the capacity of 22,000 MT per annum

Further increased the capacity to 14000 MT

2007- 8

Doubled the capacity of the acquired facility from 2000 MT to 4000 MT

Relocated manufacturing facilities from Madurai to Dadra

2003 - 4

Added new range of products to the portfolio including Nylon 6 chips, Yarns, Nylon Tire Cord, Polyester tire cord, Dipped Nylon Polyester.

2007- 8

Started a manufacturing facility at Dadra

2005 - 6

Madura Industrial Textiles division split from Madura coats Ltd and was setup as a seperate company that was taken over by Kalra Group of Companies With the entire set of manufacturing facilities and were re located from Madurai to dadra (Silvassa U.T.)
