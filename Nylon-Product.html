MITAMIDE TM PA6 / Nylon 6 - Chips

MITAMIDE TM is Medium viscosity polymer produced by MITL it has excellent mechanical properties and also has good Chemical, Heat, Oil and Abrasion Resistance. These polymers are used in various applications of technical textiles, Packaging films, Fishing Nets, automotive components and various industrial products.

MITAMIDE TM is available in various types such as Base Grade, Glass filled, Mineral filled, Lubricated, Nucleated, Copolymers and Colours master batches.

The Nylon 6 chips produced by MITL has excellent mechanical properties and also has good chemical, heat, Oil and Abrasion Resistance and are used in various applications from Technical Textiles, Packaging, Fishing Nets and Engineering Plastics for Automotive and various Industrial uses.
