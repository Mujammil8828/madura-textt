Company Profile

Established over a hundred years ago, Madura Industrial Textiles (MITL) is a trust inspiring name offering industrial fabrics for tires, conveyor belts and mechanical rubbers industry.


MITL also produces its own Nylon 6 Polymer & Nylon 6 Yarns to support its own production.


We are consistently rated amongst the top suppliers in India, Madura Industrial Textiles Ltd has two manufacturing units – DADRA PLANT & SARIGAM PLANT located in Vapi and We are consistently rated amongst the top suppliers in India, MADURA TECHNICAL FABRICS LTD has one plant in KARAJGAM, strategically located close to the Sea Port. Synonymous with innovation and long standing expertise


MITL was formerly a division of Madura Coats Limited which was acquired by the flourishing Kalra Group of Companies in 2003, a move which initiated consolidation and brought focused leadership resulting in a wave of modernization and growth for the company. With embracement of cutting edge technologies and robust investment towards advanced machinery and sequential capacity enhancements, MITL has been able to blend its experience & legacy with a vibrant, forward looking energy to emerge as an even stronger player since then.


MITL has a widely distributed and well equipped presence which enables it to meet the needs of its customers successfully offering high quality products and services to a diverse set of sectors across extended geographical regions including India, North America, South East Asia, Europe and Middle East.


Throughout this successful and continuing journey, MITL has held close to its heart a set of guiding values. One of the most important of these has been a sincere and unwavering respect for people and the relationships MITL shares with them. This has resulted in a very humane and accessible approach to work, with MITL earning its reputation as one of the most humble and approachable companies in India.


Buoyed by its success under the leadership of the Kalra Group, MITL with the same honest intent and vision for the business, is now on the verge of an ascent to even higher realms, as it undertakes significant capacity expansions and rapid diversifications through investments into new products.

HERITAGE

MITL draws from the tradition and experiences of two well-established industrial houses - Madura Coats and Kalra Group of Companies.


The farmer is a reputed name in the textile industry that has been established for over 125 year ago and the latter is a group that has over three dedicates of experience in the manufactured rubber goods industry.

PRODUCTION FACILITIES

MITL has two highly advanced manufacturing facilities at Dadra, Silvassa (U. T) and Sarigam, Gujarat making it one of the largest capacity technical textile producer in the country. While the Dadra, Silvassa (U. T) facility is one of the largest of conveyor belt fabrics upto a width of 2400 mm, this unit handle production of Specialty Fabric, The Coard and Conveyer Belt Fabrics with an annual capacity of 14,000 tonnes. the most recent addition in the Sarigam facility which manufacture superior quality Polyester and Nylon Tier Coards, Nylon Chips and Yarns and has a capacity nof 22,000 tonnes per annum.


In addition, MITL also owns and operates a highly specialised state-of-the-arts R & D Lab which spearheads the drive for continuous innovation.

QUALITY CONTROLS

MITL is an ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 & BS ISO 45001:2018 - OHSMS certified company. It follows a stringent quality control system with testing and checking systems that ensure consistency of output and world class quality.


Recently the company has introduced its own customised model for Zero Defect Programme. This initiative gas been shaped and led by the company’s senior management team and focuses on achieving higher productivity and profitability without even the slightest compromise on quality.

Complemented with its high tech precise engineering and state of the art manufacturing facilities, MITL is equipped with competent core processes for specialized technical textile products.
